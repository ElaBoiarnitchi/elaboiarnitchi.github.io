01

The Strategic Brief

Treating all customers equally is a budget leak.

The Challenge:

Retailers often waste marketing spend on one-time buyers while ignoring the VIPs who drive 40% of the profit. This project implements a behavioral framework to identify who to reward, who to upsell, and who to save.

By scoring Recency, Frequency, and Monetary impact, we move from generic newsletters to laser-targeted profit strategies.

Financial Performance Insights

Revenue Pillar

The "Champions" Segment

Insight: Although they make up only 10% of the database, they generate nearly half of all profit.

Business Action: Implement a VIP Loyalty program to shield them from competitors.

Revenue Leak

The "At Risk" Recovery

Insight: High-spending customers who haven't ordered in 90+ days. This represents a significant potential loss.

Business Action: Automated win-back campaigns with "We Miss You" personalized offers.
