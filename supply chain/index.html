01

The Strategic Brief

Moving from reactive logistics to proactive management.

The Challenge:

In global supply chains, data often sits in silos. This solution integrates Procurement, Production, and Sales to identify bottlenecks before they impact the bottom line.

The goal is to provide regional managers with actionable data to reduce freight spend and ensure that manufacturing defects never reach the end customer.

02

Actionable Business Insights

Cost Reduction

Freight Carrier Variance

Insight: DHL maintains the highest On-Time Delivery, but Maersk offers 20% lower costs for bulk shipments despite higher customs delays.

Action: Shift 15% of non-urgent cargo to Sea Freight to save ~$200k/quarter.

Inventory Optimization

Capital Recovery

Insight: Euro Hubs are overstocked by 30%, while US facilities face stockout risks on key product lines.

Action: Rebalance stock to free up $1.2M in tied-up capital and reduce holding costs.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Supply Chain Metric Business Intelligence Logic Strategic Value
On-Time Delivery (OTD) % of shipments delivered on or before promise. Primary driver of customer retention and brand trust.
Defect Rate % (Units failed QA / Total Produced) * 100. Directly impacts COGS; prevents "Bad Batches" from shipping.
Stock-to-Safety Ratio Current Stock / Safety Stock. Maintains agility; prevents lost sales from stockouts.
Lead Time Variance Actual Delivery Date - Promised Date. Identifies unreliable suppliers to renegotiate contracts.
